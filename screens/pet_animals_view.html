Animaux Domestiques Exotiques

Ici vous découvrirez et en apprendre plus sur tous les animaux domestiques hors du commun et exotiques en fonction de leur catégorie.

Album Fish

 

Poisson-clown

e poisson clown ou amphiprion fait partie d'une famille comprenant elle-même une trentaine de sous-espèces. Mondialement connu pour avoir inspiré Disney et son personnage "Némo", le poisson clown dont l'aire de répartition se situe principalement dans l'océan indien, est un animal pacifique qui entretien une relation privilégiée avec les anémones de mer.

9 mins

Chirurgien bleu

Parcourant les eaux tièdes de l'océan indo-pacifique jusqu'à l'Australie, le chirurgien bleu est un poisson très facilement reconnaissable à sa couleur azur , ainsi qu'à l'extrémité jaune de sa queue.

9 mins

Mickey Mouse Platy

Platys are colorful, live-bearing fish that are very suitable for beginners. Since they are live-bearing fish, many hobbyists start out with only a few fish—which can quickly multiply into many fish. The Mickey Mouse Platy is named for a very specific color pattern formed in the tail peduncle.

9 mins

Guppies

Guppies are a cornerstone of the small fish aquarium hobby. Another live-bearing species, guppy tanks are often overrun with offspring from frequent breeding. The best skill any guppy owner can learn is to tell the difference between males and females to keep populations from exploding. The males are smaller and more colorful fish, the larger females can have a colorful tail fin, but the body is plain with a large white belly.

9 mins

Zebra danio (Zebrafish)

These small, striped fish are one of the few in the aquarium hobby that don't require warm tropical temperatures. Zebrafish are peaceful, community players that love schooling in groups of 5 or more fish.

9 mins

Neon Tetra

One of the most common small freshwater aquarium species is the Neon Tetra. These fish are known for their colorful appearance with bright red and blue stripes. They are great community fish and school together in a vibrant wave.

9 mins

Harlequin Rasbora

The Harlequin Rasbora is a small, schooling fish with a vibrant ruby coloration, contrasted with a black marking on the back half of its body, giving it the two-toned 'harlequin' appearance.

9 mins

Glowlight Tetra

The Glowlight Tetra is not to be confused with GloFish species. Their coloration comes from natural color patterns and has not been genetically engineered. They are a peaceful schooling tetra that is easy to care for.

9 mins

Dwarf Gourami

Of the many gourami species, there are few that stay small. Most gouramis are medium-sized fish, but the Dwarf Gourami, as the name suggests, tops out at only 2 inches. Like other gouramis, these intermediate-level fishes have specific environmental requirements for successful lives.

9 mins

Connemara

Apprécié pour sa polyvalence comme cheval de loisir, ou de dressage, le connemara dont les qualités excellent au saut d'obstacle, est un poney équilibré, au pas sur, qui conviendra parfaitement aux enfants et rassurera les parents, tant il est doux et facile a éduquer

9 mins

Frison

A la fois utilisé comme cheval de selle et de demi-trait, le frison, originaire des pays bas est un grand cheval à la formidable musculature, dont la robe est toujours noire.

9 mins

Mustang

Assez indépendant, mais tout de même domesticable, le mustang de par ses origines est un cheval de taille moyenne, plutôt rustique, et aux robes variées.

9 mins

Quarter-Horse

Présent dans les ranchs et accompagnant les cowboys pour diriger le bétail, le Quarter-Horse qui a fait les beaux jours des westerns est également un cheval très intelligent, sociable et loyal dont le caractère est fort apprécié.

9 mins

Selle Français

Doté d'une croupe musclée et puissante et d'un dos droit, le selle Français, dont l'allure est remarquable est un cheval généreux, intelligent et vif.

9 mins

Brumby

Reconnu aujourd'hui comme cheval sauvage et vivant en très grand nombre sur le continent australien, le brumby est considéré comme nuisible par les éleveurs et par l'état australien.

9 mins

Appaloosa

De taille moyenne et ne dépassant pas les 470 kg, l'appaloosa est une valeur sure, mais est plutôt réservé à des cavaliers expérimentés.

9 mins

Akhal téké

Considéré comme le plus beau cheval au monde, l'akhal téké doré est un cheval multidisciplinaire qui se fera remarquer aussi bien dans les concours de dressage, que dans les concours complets ou les épreuves d'endurance.

9 mins

Clydesdale

tilisé pour remonter le charbon des mines au 19ème siècle, le clydesdale utilisé aujourd'hui comme cheval de trait ou de compagnie est un cheval robuste au caractère très doux, assez facile à dresser.

9 mins

African Grey Parrot

African Grey parrots are the most heavily studied species of pet birds and have demonstrated a very high level of intelligence.

9 mins

Macaws

Also considered parrots like the African Grey, there are several different types of macaws, including the longest parrot on the planet, the Hyacinth macaw. All parrots, and therefore macaws, are thought to have similar levels of intelligence.

9 mins

Cockatoos

The cockatoo, also a type of parrot, was the first type of bird to be seen making musical instruments. In Australia, palm cockatoos were observed using twigs and seed pods to make drumsticks, and other types of cockatoos can sway to a musical beat, demonstrating they have an understanding of rhythm.

9 mins

Budgerigars

More commonly referred to as budgies or the common parakeet, the budgerigar is one of the smallest members of the parrot family (with the parrotlet being the actual smallest parrot). But just because it's small, doesn't mean that the budgie isn't smart.

9 mins

Conures

Like the other types of birds on this list, there are many different kinds of conures and all conures are types of parrots. Because of this, conures also require a lot of mental stimulation and enrichment to keep their smart brains busy. A lot of exercises, toys, and social activities are a must for a conure.

9 mins

Cockatiel

he captivating cockatiel is another great choice for anyone who wants a friendly and affectionate pet bird. Hailing from Australia, these birds make excellent pets when hand-fed as babies and raised in positive environments.

9 mins

Parrotlet

punky and relatively easy to care for, parrotlets are known for having personalities much larger than their tiny bodies. Those considering parrotlets must have plenty of time to play and socialize with their pets, as these birds can become a little wild without regular handling.

9 mins

Canary

Known for the sweet singing of the males, canaries are friendly birds that are a type of finch native to the Canary Islands. They are cheerful and intelligent small birds that can be easily trained to sit on their owner's hand, and they are easy to care for, making them a good beginner's bird.

9 mins

Eclectus Parrot

Native to rainforests in the Solomon Islands, the eclectus parrot is a species with striking dimorphism, meaning the males and females look very different; the males are bright green while the females are red. These large parrots are very friendly and intelligent, but do best in a home without too much hustle and bustle, as they are easily stressed by noise and tumult.

9 mins

Pet Rabbit

Rabbits can make wonderful pets. Like other rabbits, they do require some space to play and exercise as well as fresh green vegetables on a daily basis, but if you are looking for an interactive small pet with a personality, rabbits are worth the work.

9 mins

Hamsters

Hamsters make great first pets, especially for kids, because they're cute and small and relatively easy to care for.

9 mins

Spiders

Not all spiders do well in captivity. Active hunters are usually easier to keep than web builders.

9 mins

Pet snail

There is more than one type of snail in the world, but the garden snail is the species that has quickly grown in popularity as a pet

9 mins

Guinea Pigs

Guinea pigs are very popular small pets and are great for both kids and adults. Guinea pigs are personable, don't take up a huge amount of space, and are usually very gentle.

9 mins

Pet Rats

Rats are highly intelligent creatures, and they enjoy your company. Hold yours in your hands and play gently, in short spurts at first until it's tamer. Keep your rat's cage in a quiet, safe area, so it always feels comfortable.

9 mins

Hedgehog

Hedgehogs are omnivores or insectivores, depending on where you look it up. This means their primary food should be made up of insect protein (chitin) and a variety of fruits, vegetables, cooked meats, live insects (such as mealworms) and more.

9 mins

Ferrets

When it comes to domesticated pet ferrets (not to be confused with wild European polecats or North American black-footed ferrets), there is actually only one breed—despite some being referred to as Angora, European, or Standard breeds.

9 mins

Tortoises

Pet tortoises are popular pets for many people since they are quiet, cute (especially as hatchlings), and don't shed any fur. But tortoises can live a very long time (anywhere from 50 to 100 years). If you take one as a pet, be prepared to provide a lifetime of care and consider that your pet might even outlive you.

9 mins
